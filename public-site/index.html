Features

Everything your team needs to manage passwords.

From everyday autofill to organization-wide control — without ever exposing a secret.

Autofill & capture

The browser extension fills saved logins and offers to save new ones as you sign in — including multi-step (email-then-password) logins.

Zero-knowledge

Everything is encrypted on your device before it reaches the server. Your master password never leaves your browser.

Secure team sharing

Share a login re-encrypted just for the recipient. Grant "use-only" access so they can log in without ever seeing the password.

Folders & generator

Keep large vaults tidy with folders, tags and favorites, plus a built-in strong-password generator and strength meter.

Admin controls

Invite and manage users, assign roles, deactivate accounts instantly, and securely transfer credentials when people leave.

Audit & reports

A full audit log plus dashboards for password health and team activity — visibility without exposing any secret.

Security

Built so that even we can't read your passwords.

E-Vault is zero-knowledge by design — your data is encrypted and decrypted only on your device.

  • Master password derived locally with PBKDF2-SHA256 (310,000 iterations).
  • Every item encrypted with AES-256-GCM; sharing uses RSA-OAEP 2048.
  • The server only ever stores encrypted ciphertext — never your keys.
  • No tracking, no ads. The extension touches credential fields only when you autofill or save.

Browser extension

Right where you log in.

Install once for Chrome or Edge. It connects automatically when you're signed in to E-Vault — no pairing codes.

  • One-click autofill and auto-login on the sites you've saved.
  • Smart capture that offers to save or update credentials as you sign in.
  • Stays unlocked for your session and locks when you close the browser.

How it works

Up and running in three steps.

1

Get invited

Your admin invites you by email. You set one password that logs you in and unlocks your vault — your keys are generated on your device.

2

Install the extension

Add E-Vault to Chrome or Edge. It connects automatically when you're signed in to the app.

3

Browse normally

E-Vault autofills your saved logins and offers to save new ones — securely, across your whole team.
